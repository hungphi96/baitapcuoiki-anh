Vietnamese calligraphy club

  • Do you love calligraphy?
  • Do you want to meet and exchange your passion for calligraphy with people with similar interests?
  • Do you want to compose calligraphy works?

Come to the Vietnamese Calligraphy Club, the club is a voluntary organization that gathers all individuals who love prefer, passionate, capable and dedicated to developing the art of Vietnamese calligraphy. Therefore, this is a useful and creative cultural and artistic playground with the goal of learning and theoretical research on art Vietnamese calligraphy because The common purpose is to serve the development of this art, improve spiritual life according to values of Truthfulness - Compassion - Beauty - Peace, contribute to preserving and promoting the nation's traditional culture.
